History

In early 2019, I began working on this project in hopes of creating a 'true' virtual world, where not only terrain but also live weather, climate zone, accurate flora, fauna and timezone would be rendered based on a physical location.

1. Timezone 2. Live weather 3. Flora and fauna accurate to climate zone and season 4. Terrain from DEM (rivers, lakes and oceans also to be rendered, other terrain details may be considered)
* DEM: Digital Elevation Model
